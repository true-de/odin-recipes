Wine Ingredients

  • Grapes
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Sulfites (as a preservative)

Steps:

  1. Harvest ripe grapes and crush them to release the juice.
  2. Add yeast to the juice to begin fermentation.
  3. Allow the fermentation process to occur for several weeks or months, depending on the type of wine.
  4. Once fermentation is complete, strain the wine and store it in barrels or bottles for aging.
  5. After aging, filter the wine and add sulfites to preserve it.
  6. Bottle the wine and allow it to age further if needed before consumption.
