Smoothie Bowl

Description

Cool, refreshing smoothie bowl topped with granola, nuts, and fruits â€“ perfect for a healthy breakfast or snack.

Smoothie Bowl Ingredients

  • Frozen Mango
  • Frozen Banana
  • Almond Milk
  • Granola
  • Chia Seeds
  • Fresh Berries

Steps:

  1. In a blender, combine frozen mango, banana, and almond milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with granola, chia seeds, and fresh berries.
  5. Serve immediately for a refreshing treat.
