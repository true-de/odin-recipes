Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes that make the perfect breakfast. Serve with syrup, butter, and your favorite toppings!

Pancake Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat and grease with butter.
  5. Pour batter onto the griddle in small circles and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
