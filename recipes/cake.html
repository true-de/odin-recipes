Cake Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs with sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add softened butter and vanilla extract to the egg mixture and beat until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, alternating with milk, mixing until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into greased cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the cakes cool before frosting or serving.
