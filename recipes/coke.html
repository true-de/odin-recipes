Coke Ingredients

  • Carbonated Water
  • High Fructose Corn Syrup
  • Caramel Color
  • Phosphoric Acid
  • Caffeine
  • Natural Flavors
  • Citric Acid

Steps:

  1. Combine carbonated water and syrup in a precise ratio.
  2. Add caramel color for the signature dark appearance.
  3. Mix in phosphoric acid and citric acid to add acidity and enhance flavor.
  4. Add caffeine and natural flavors to create the distinct taste.
  5. Package and carbonate the drink to perfection.
